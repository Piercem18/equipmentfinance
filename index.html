OUR FINANCIAL PROGRAMS

Loans and Leases Financing Options

Our financing solutions encompass loans and leases tailored to your specific needs. Recognizing that each option carries its own set of advantages and disadvantages, we offer detailed guidance to help you make an informed choice for your business. We are committed to securing the best rates, thus optimizing your ROI.

Loan

A loan is a flexible financing solution that not only allows you to access modern equipment but also grants ownership, along with the potential deductions of loan interest and equipment depreciation from taxable income, enhancing cost-effectiveness.

Lease

A lease is a cost-effective financing option that offers preserved cash flow and potential tax benefits through deductible lease payments and depreciation write-offs. Both Operational and Capital leases available.

Refinance

Refinancing involves strategic options like leasing equipment shortly after purchase or selling to a lender and leasing back, which can unlock cash tied up in assets and provide flexibility for reinvestment, all while potentially benefiting from lower interest rates.

Interim Funding Option

Interim funding, such as a gap loan, is a unique solution common in manufacturing, where clients start making payments only on interest even before the equipment is shipped, with the financing converting into a lease once installed.
